**EXECUTIVE SUMMARY**

Mumbai's ready-to-eat healthy snacks market is competitive and dynamic, with **EatFit** and **Slurrp Farm** being prominent players. While EatFit leads with a wider range of protein-rich snacks and better review counts, SlurrpFarm offers unique value in whole grains and smoothie mixes.

Customers appreciate the product quality (EatFit) and goodness (SlurrpFarm). However, both struggle with stock issues and high pricing concerns (SlurrpFarm).

To strengthen their market position, both companies should focus on **vegan-specific products**, improving **launch promotions** discounts, and offering clearer information about **stock limitations**. Diversifying **gluten-free** and **organic** ranges could also attract conscious consumers.

1. **Product Expansion into Vegan-specific Lines**
   - Introduce dedicated vegan-friendly products (High Impact)

2. **Improve Launch Promotions**
   - Offer larger discounts on new products or bundle packs (Medium Impact)
   - Encourages purchases, attracts price-sensitive customers (Medium Effort)
